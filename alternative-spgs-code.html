Spot

Innovators and members of the ecosystem spot pressing challenges with huge social impact and brainstorm to generate an idea that addresses the challenge.

Probe

Teams from around the world take part in focused workshops and camps to develop rough functioning proofs of concepts.

Grow

Innovation centers and partners contribute their talents, skills and resources to evolve the prototypes into more polished, validated fully functioning devices.

Scale

Host organization consortium members, multinationals and a network of global partners facilitate the successful deployment of the devices.
