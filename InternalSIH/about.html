What is Internal Smart India Hackathon


Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving. In SIH, the students would have the opportunity to work on challenges faced within various Ministries, Departments, Industries, PSUs and NGOs to create world class solutions for some of the top organizations including industries in the world, thus helping the Private sector hire the best minds from across the nation

All participants with different levels of experience, professional and academic backgrounds, diverse skill sets were part of the teams; what matters most was their commitment to making an impact and willingness to collaborate.



Objectives of Internal SIH


  1. To harness creativity and expertise of students.
  2. Providing spark to institute level Hackathon.
  3. To support the massive Startup India campaign.
  4. Crowd source solutions to improve governance and to raise the quality of life of the citizens.
  5. To provide an opportunity to get innovative solutions to India's daunting problems.
