What Is We4Tech

“We4Tech” is a one-of-a-kind initiative by REDX labs & ‘InnoWe’ to promote innovative solutions for addressing key challenges faced by Businesses and society at large. We4tech is an event in which participants with different backgrounds and expertise form teams, collaborate within a limited time frame, and focus on a specific problem or idea/ challenge to come up with innovative solutions. Since 2022, We4Tech has been integrated into WeSchool's foundation program, engaging all FY students, and competing fiercely for top honours and runner-up positions.

Objectives of We4Tech

  1. To sensitize participants and make them compete amongst one another as a team and offer technical solutions to real-world problems.
  2. To boost the innovation culture and further establish idea-sharing, effective collaboration and creativeness driven by enthusiasm.
  3. Great opportunity to discover & learn new technologies, and teams and demonstrate their skills and talents outside their typical management competitions.
  4. Opportunity to identify talent, experience powerful teams being set up, capture valuable feedback and make participants aware of their capabilities and thus bolster participant's confidence, which is highly desirable for students.
  5. To generate high-value actionable business ideas and product concepts and have a functional prototype/business solution by the end of the hackathon.
