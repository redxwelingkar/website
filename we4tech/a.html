Tech 1: 3D Modeling and Printing

Our objective with this tech is to sensitize the participants with the world of 3D modeling and printing innovation and explore its potential to democratize the production of goods, from food to medical supplies, to the manufacturing industry as a whole. In the future, 3D printing machines could make their way into homes, businesses, disaster sites, and even outer space. We are looking for participants to put on their thinking hats and understand the scalability and usability of 3D printing to solve real world problems.
